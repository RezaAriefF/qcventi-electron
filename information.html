Information

Quality Control Ventilator functions to check the workings of the ventilator that will be used in the hospital whether it is in accordance with the actual function in terms of accuracy and precision, because every mechanical ventilator needs routine maintenance and also needs periodic calibration so that with the Quality Control of this ventilator, we hope can make it easier for every agency that owns or works with ventilators so that they can routinely carry out independent calibrations. The Quality Control Ventilator itself can also function like a ventilator if needed in urgent conditions so it can increase the efficiency of productivity related medical activities to help patients or people who need help breathing, the use of this tool should be carried out by professionals in their fields and must read the manual first .
